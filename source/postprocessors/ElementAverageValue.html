ElementAverageValue

Computes the volumetric average of a variable

The element average of a variable is defined as the ratio of its integral over the volume of the domain:

This average may be taken over a restriction of blocks, but for taking an average over boundaries, a SideAverageValue should be used instead.

Example input syntax

In this example, we compute the average of a variable over the whole domain.

[Postprocessors]
  [./average]
    type = ElementAverageValue
    variable = u
  [../]
[]
(moose/test/tests/postprocessors/element_average_value/element_average_value_test.i)
